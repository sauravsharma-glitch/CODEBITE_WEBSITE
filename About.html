our story

Codebite was born in 2022 out of a small but passionate conversation between two developers who believed that learning and building should feel like a bite-sized joy. What started as a tiny blog to share code snippets quickly turned into a growing community of curious coders, freelancers, and tech enthusiasts across India.

Today, codebite.co.in is where ideas meet execution. We don't just write code — we craft solutions, share insights, and help businesses take their first (or next) digital leap. Our journey is rooted in curiosity, clean code, and human connection.
